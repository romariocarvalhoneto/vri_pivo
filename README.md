# VRI Pivo
Center Pivot Systems Irrigation

The plugin performs data interpolation and delineation of management zones intended for variable rate irrigation (VRI) management in center pivot systems.

The need for the rational use of natural resources has driven the search for more efficient management strategies in irrigated agriculture. In this context, precision irrigation based on management zones emerges as a promising alternative to increase water and energy efficiency without compromising agricultural productivity. 

The plugin had the following objectives: (i) to automate the stages of interpolation, processing, and delineation of management zones intended for central pivot irrigation; and (ii) to adapt a method for defining regularly shaped management zones (“pizza slices”) for central pivot irrigation based on variations in water application rate through equipment speed control, as an alternative to pixel-based rate variation approaches or zones with irregular shapes. The developed plugin was capable of executing the workflow, facilitating the entire process of delineating management zones for irrigation purposes and providing a free and open-source platform, combined with the functions already available in QGIS.
